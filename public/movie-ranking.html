My Top 3 of All Time.

Anjaam Pathira

An unique psychotic crime thriller from Malayalam Industry based on the service of police officers in Kerala. It takes a huge turn when a police officer is murdered. It has lot of twists which makes it a superhit.

Fidaa

Fidaa is a romantic Telugu movie which highlights Indian traditions and culture. This is a feel good movie which shows the hindrances in love and the way people handle it.

Hridhayam

A feel good Malayalam movie which shows how life flows and its magic.
